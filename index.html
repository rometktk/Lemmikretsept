Lemmikretsept: Puder

Ingredient Amount
Rolled oats 100 g
Water or milk 250 ml
Salt (optional) 2 g
Cinnamon 2 g
Bannanas 100 g
  1. Step 1 Boil the liquid: In a pot, bring 250 ml of water or milk to a boil.
  2. Step 2 Add oats: Stir in 100 g of rolled oats and 2 g of salt (optional).
  3. Step 3 Cook: Reduce heat to low and simmer for 5â€“10 minutes, stirring occasionally until the oats are soft and thickened.
  4. Step 4 Add cinnamon: Stir in 2 g of cinnamon.
  5. Step 5 Top with bananas: Slice 100 g of bananas and place them on top of the cooked oatmeal.
  6. Step 6 Serve: Enjoy!
